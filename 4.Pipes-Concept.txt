Pipes Concept Based Learning
============================
- Pipes are the concept/tool that used to combine two commands where one command output is piped as an argument to the
  next command.
- Pipes denoted as |
- Syntax:
	$ command1 [args] | command2 [args]
- Example:
	$ mkdir FolderName | touch FileName.ext | find ./Fold* -name FileN*.ext
